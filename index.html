About Dr. Ari Nunes

Dr. Ari Nunes is a Doctor of Acupuncture and Chinese Medicine with over a decade of experience in holistic healthcare. Originally from Brazil, he studied at the Atlantic Institute of Oriental Medicine in Florida and trained in Hangzhou, China to deepen his expertise. He later earned his doctoral degree in Acupuncture with a specialization in Chinese Herbal Medicine from the Colorado Chinese Medicine University.

Dr. Nunes views health holistically, treating the root causes of imbalances rather than just symptoms. His approach blends acupuncture, herbal medicine, whole-food supplements, homeopathy, and lifestyle adjustments to restore harmony and vitality.

"Healing begins when we align with the wisdom within, allowing the body, mind, and spirit to flow in harmony with the universe."
